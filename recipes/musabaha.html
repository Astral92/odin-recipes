Musabaha

Description

Musabaha is the lesser known cousin of hummus (the dip) but is just as delicious and easier to make too.

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas
  • Hot water
  • Tahini
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Spice (addition)
  • Parsely (addition)

Steps

  1. Mince the clove of garlic in a bowl.
  2. Add the salt to the garlic. Then add some of the olive oil,the tahini and lemon juice.
  3. Gently mash around half of the chickpeas with the back of the fork (don't pulverise them) just enough to break them up
  4. Plate up and drizzle parsely and your prefered spice
